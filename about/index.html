About me

I’m a Product Support Specialist at Syntech Systems (FuelMaster), promoted from Level 1 to Level 2. I resolve customer-impacting issues across FuelMaster Live and Legacy—SSH into embedded Linux, trace logs through OSGi services, isolate root causes, and coordinate escalations and migrations when fixes require broader alignment.

I care about outcomes and the details that produce them: I hit 105% of goal in my first year (2nd in the department) and maintain a 97.2% CSAT against a 95% target.

I’m moving toward product development because I enjoy building software. I’ve been developing a Java/Spring Boot portfolio application using a layered architecture with Spring Data JPA and an H2 database, and I’m building out an Angular front end. What I like most is turning messy real-world requirements into something clear, testable, and maintainable.

Outside of work, I’m an avid mountain biker, married, and a dad of two. I also spent six years as a stay-at-home parent, which sharpened prioritization, patience, and staying calm under pressure—skills that translate well into technical support and engineering work.
